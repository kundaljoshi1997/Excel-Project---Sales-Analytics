# Sales & Finance Analysis Project

## Project Overview

This project focuses on end-to-end Sales and Financial Performance Analysis using Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX. The objective is to transform raw business data into meaningful, decision-ready insights through well-structured reports.

---

## Sales Report

### Objectives

* Develop a comprehensive Customer Performance Report.
* Perform an in-depth comparison of Market Performance vs. Sales Targets.

### Purpose of Sales Analytics

Enable businesses to monitor, evaluate, and optimize their sales performance using data-driven insights.

### Why Sales Data Analysis Matters

* Identify sales trends and recurring patterns.
* Track and evaluate key performance indicators (KPIs).

### Role of Sales Reports

* Design effective customer discount strategies.
* Support data-backed negotiations with customers.
* Identify high-potential countries for business expansion.

---

## Finance Report

### Objectives

* Create Profit & Loss (P&L) Reports by:

  * Fiscal Year
  * Month

Evaluate financial performance, support strategic decision-making, and enhance communication with stakeholders.
